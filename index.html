Our Story

Building Hawaii's
Heart

Masterpiece Construction & Development was founded on a simple belief: A house and a community can have a heart, and can last generations. In order to do this, we take the time to understand how the community moves, how they live, what they value, and what legacy they will leave behind — all before the pencil hits paper.

We ask: What becomes of it 20 years after the bulldozers are gone? Is it still Thriving and Beautiful? Do our Children Feel safe?

With deep roots in the Hawaiian Islands and a new expanding presence in San Antonio, Texas, we bring boutique-level attention to every project — from intimate custom residences to master-planned luxury communities. We navigate Hawaii's unique land conditions, entitlement hurdles, permitting landscape, and island building codes with confidence that only comes from years of hands-on experience.

Land Entitlement &
Ground Up MF/SF

Transforming raw land into buildable lots that set the stage for exceptional construction. We manage every step of the programming complexities — Environmental Impact, Traffic, Entitlement, County Approvals, FEMA, ACOE, Parks, Civil, Stormwater, all Utilities, School, Police and Fire — so that by the time a shovel breaks ground, the land is ready and the path is clear.

Our ground-up multifamily and single-family expertise means we don't just entitle land — we envision, design, and deliver the community from first concept to final certificate of occupancy. We have done it across Hawaii's most complex regulatory landscapes and emerged stronger every time.

South Maui, Hawaii

Kenolio Village

South Maui, Hawaii

A 240-unit affordable housing community in the heart of South Maui's Kenolio district — a culturally significant coastal landscape where an active estuary meets the Pacific. This project demands, and receives, the highest level of environmental stewardship: complex floodwater mitigation, estuary preservation protocols, and deep community stakeholder engagement are integral to our entitlement process.

South Maui needs housing. Kenolio Village is our commitment to building it responsibly — thoughtfully planned, culturally respectful, and designed to serve generations of Maui families.
